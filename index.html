about me

marcie shields

About ten years ago I started my yoga studies at an ashram in India. There I discovered the capabilities of our body physically and energetically and something in me ignited. Growing up dancing and traveling in the ballet gave me a perspective and appreciation on art and athleticism at a young age. I am now a mother of two and have gone through many changes, setbacks and transformations with my own health. Currently an athlete of bodybuilding in my 40’s I am a strong believer that personal power never depletes. I am enthusiastic about helping other people get in touch with their own personal power and transformative energy. I aspire to work with all people, from children to seniors, men and women. I have no niche group or one size fits all programming. I see everyone as an individual and work with people from the inside out. I believe the way you look is only the byproduct of the work you put in, but it’s such a reward when you’ve found your way on your path!
